Who am I ?

I am a young guy but with very big ambitions. My name is Alexandru Soltan and I am 20 years old. I have chosen multimedia design because it is an opportunity for me to learn a lot and to improve my skills

My story.

I was born in Republic of Moldova and after I graduated gymnasium I started lyceum in Romania where I received a lot of experience. I was always inspired by everything and I love art. I have chosen Multimedia Design because it is a kind of art and you can create something great from nothing. Last six months I started my new adventure in Denmark. I met a lot of people and every day I have to learn something new. I believe that step by step I can change the world.

The desired future.

I hope in the near future I will travel around the world, I will discover new for me cultures and will meet a lot of friends. I hope I will have my own company and I will work with what I like. I hope I will be a great multimedia designer.
